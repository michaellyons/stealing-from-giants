# Book Structure

## Proposed Outline (v0.1)

### Part I: The Beginning
1. **Introduction** — Who I was, why I started
2. **The Decision** — Quitting the old path, betting on yourself
3. **Day One** — First lines of code, imposter syndrome

### Part II: The Method
4. **Stealing from Giants** — The philosophy explained
5. **Learning to Learn** — How to teach yourself when no one taught you how
6. **The Projects** — Building things that don't exist yet

### Part III: The Transition
7. **The Job Hunt** — Resumes, interviews, proving yourself
8. **First Year** — What they don't tell you about professional coding
9. **The Gap** — What CS grads know that you don't (and how to catch up)

### Part IV: The Lessons
10. **What Actually Matters** — Skills vs. credentials
11. **Mentorship & Community** — Finding your people
12. **The Long Game** — Continuing to grow

### Part V: For the Next Generation
13. **Actionable Guide** — How to do what I did
14. **Resources** — The actual tools, courses, and communities
15. **Final Thoughts** — It's never too late
